Doctor Sleep

Doctor Sleep is a sequel to The Shining. It's plot is centered around the only Survivor of the Shining events at the Outlook Hotel,Danny Torrance. After becoming an orphan and fighting his trauma of his dads violence, shame and alcoholism he settled as a nurse at a retaierment home. His crucial "shining" ability that allows the elderly to pass over n their final moments segways him to Abra Stone. A child with a far more powerful form of the "shining" than Dan and any other wielder before. As Abra is endlessly fleeing from "The True Knot", an unknowable ancient group of hostile wielders that wants Abra's "steam" a substance that exits a wielders body as they are tortured slowly to death, for the Knot to consume that prolongs their immortality. Danny is the only one that can protect her. fandom.com
